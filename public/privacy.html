ASCEND Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2026
✓ No ads · No tracking · No data selling

ASCEND ("the App") is a daily brain-training game developed by Mustafa Boudjerada. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account Data (optional): If you choose to create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used only for authentication
  • Display name — shown on leaderboards
  • Chosen path — your selected player identity

Game Data: When signed in, we store your game progress in the cloud:

  • XP totals, streak counts, challenge results
  • Duel history and leaderboard ranking

No account required: You can play ASCEND without creating an account. In that case, all data is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No contacts or phone data
  • ❌ No photos, camera, or microphone access
  • ❌ No advertising identifiers
  • ❌ No analytics or usage tracking
  • ❌ No cookies (beyond auth session)
  • ❌ No third-party SDKs for advertising or tracking

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Sync your game progress across devices
  • Display your ranking on the global leaderboard
  • Enable async duels with other players

4. Data Storage & Security

All cloud data is stored securely on Supabase (powered by PostgreSQL) with:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) — users can only access their own data
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Hosted in the US (East) on secure infrastructure

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Period.

The only publicly visible data is your display name, path, and XP on the leaderboard — which you choose to display when you create an account.

6. Children's Privacy

ASCEND is suitable for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account — use the "Reset Progress" button in the Profile tab, or submit a formal request via our Account Deletion page
  • Play without an account — skip auth entirely, zero data leaves your device
  • Request data export — contact us at the email below

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
